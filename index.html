The Artist Behind the Lens

I'm a photographer based in Maryland, available across the DMV area and beyond.

I got into photography because I love people — the way they laugh, the way they look at each other, the small moments that happen in between the big ones. Those are the moments I chase.

My style is natural and candid. I'm not big on stiff poses or forced smiles. I'd rather blend into the background and let things unfold naturally — and more often than not, that's where the best shots come from.

Whether it's your wedding day, a portrait session, or a special event, I'll be there to capture it honestly and beautifully.
